Frequently Asked
Is my data private and secure? +
Yes. All conversations are end-to-end encrypted. We never sell your data. You can delete all your history anytime from settings. We're GDPR compliant and follow Indian data protection laws.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes — no lock-ins, no cancellation fees. Cancel from settings in one click. You keep access until your billing period ends.
Does this replace real doctors or lawyers? +
No. Zenvy AI provides guidance to help you understand your situation and prepare better. For serious legal or medical matters, always consult a licensed professional. Think of us as your preparation layer.
Does it work in Hindi? +
Yes! Type in Hindi, Hinglish, or English. Our agents respond in whatever language you prefer.
Which payment methods are accepted? +
UPI, debit/credit cards, net banking, Razorpay (India) and Stripe (International). All major payment methods supported.
